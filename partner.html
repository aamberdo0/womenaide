Women's Habitat

"Women's Habitat is extremely grateful to WomenAide for their support in providing abused women and their children with much-needed donations. Items such as shampoo, toothpaste, diapers and hand lotion may seem like common, everyday items to us - but to a woman escaping abuse, it is one less thing that she has to pay for out of her already stretched budget. Please know that your donations are used and very much appreciated."

Ernestine's Women's Shelter

WomenAide’s passion to help women and children fleeing domestic violence is evident in the quality of items they have provided to our shelter. When victims leave our shelter they have to start over, which can be quite costly. WomenAide has displayed great thoughtfulness and consideration in donating items to us which every women needs, but not all can afford. Thank you WomenAide for your commitment to supporting women and children escaping violence.”
